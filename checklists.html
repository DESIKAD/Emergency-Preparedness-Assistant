Emergency Essentials Checklist

Earthquake Essentials:

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for at least 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food (canned goods, energy bars, etc.)
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Portable power bank (for charging phones)
  • Emergency blanket or warm clothing
  • Dust mask (to filter air if necessary)
  • Multi-tool or manual can opener
  • Important documents (in a waterproof container)
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio

Tsunami Essentials:

  • Water (at least 3 daysâ€™ worth)
  • Non-perishable food
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First-aid kit
  • Emergency contact numbers and documents
  • Portable power bank
  • Warm clothing and emergency blanket
  • Lifejacket (if near water)
  • Emergency whistle (for signaling help)

Fire Accident Essentials:

  • Water and non-perishable snacks
  • Fire extinguisher (appropriate for the type of fire)
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • Smoke mask or wet cloth (to cover nose/mouth)
  • Evacuation route plan
  • Phone charger and portable power bank
  • Wet towels or fire-resistant clothing

Flood Essentials:

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for at least 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food (canned goods, energy bars)
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Waterproof clothing and boots
  • Life-saving tools (whistle, rope, etc.)
  • Emergency waterproof bags for documents
  • Portable power bank
  • Plastic bags (for sealing valuables)

Landslide Essentials:

  • Water (for at least 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Heavy-duty gloves (for handling debris)
  • Emergency whistle (for signaling help)
  • Emergency contact numbers and documents
  • Sturdy footwear (for walking on uneven ground)
  • Portable power bank

Tornado Essentials:

  • Water (at least 1 gallon per person per day for 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • Portable power bank
  • Sturdy shoes and gloves
  • Heavy-duty blanket or shelter supplies
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • Whistle or signal flare
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio

Storm Essentials:

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First-aid kit
  • Emergency contact information
  • Battery-powered radio or weather alerts
  • Phone charger and portable power bank
  • Sturdy shoes, clothing, and blanket
  • Sandbags to prevent flooding

Hurricane Essentials:

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for 3-7 days)
  • Non-perishable food (at least 3 days' worth)
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • First-aid kit
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
  • Phone charger and portable power bank
  • Waterproof clothing and blankets
  • Sandbags and plastic sheeting for waterproofing
  • Important documents in a waterproof bag

Drought Essentials:

  • Water (at least 1 gallon per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • Water purification tablets or filters
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • Phone charger and portable power bank
  • Sunscreen and protective clothing
  • Emergency contact information

Heatwave Essentials:

  • Water (stay hydrated)
  • Non-perishable food
  • Sunscreen and protective clothing (wide-brimmed hat)
  • Battery-powered fan or cooling devices
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Portable power bank
  • Emergency contact information

Volcanic Eruption Essentials:

  • Water (at least 3 days' worth)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Dust mask to protect from ash
  • Portable power bank
  • Emergency contact numbers and documents
  • Sturdy footwear (to navigate through ash)
  • Emergency radio or alerts
